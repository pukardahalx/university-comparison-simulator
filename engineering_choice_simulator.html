Engineering Choice Simulator

A multi-criteria analysis justifying the structural pivot to University of Padua

The Baseline Thesis: Application development is already a checked box in my personal stack. Re-studying abstract computer science risks redundancy. Selecting the University of Padua (Information Engineering) transitions skillset directly into physical systems layer engineering, mathematical optimization, and low-level computer hardware architectures.
Methodology & Data Sourcing: The baseline scores (0-100%) for each university are not arbitrary. They are calculations derived from comparing the required European Credit Transfer System (ECTS/CFU) allocations between the Italian L-8 macro-class (Engineering) and L-31 macro-class (Computer Science). For example, a program requiring 30 CFUs in physics and analog circuits scores heavily in "Hardware", while a program replacing those with 30 CFUs in Machine Learning scores heavily in "Software".
